---
title: "Customization"
description: "Theme, brand, and configure the deposit widget to match your app."
---

The deposit and withdraw modals accept theming and branding props to match your app's look and feel.

## Theme

Control the visual appearance of the modal: light or dark mode, accent color, font color, icon color, border color, background color, and border radius. Pass these via the `theme` prop.

## Branding

Set a custom logo and title displayed in the modal header via the `branding` prop.

## UI configuration

Toggle specific UI elements on or off: logo visibility, stepper visibility, back button, and deposit amount limits (min/max USD). Pass these via the `uiConfig` prop.
