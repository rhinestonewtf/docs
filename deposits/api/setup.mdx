---
title: "Setup"
description: "Configure your Deposit API client with webhooks, sponsorship rules, and deposit whitelists."
---

Before processing deposits, you need to configure your client with the deposit service. This is a one-time step that sets up how you receive notifications, which fees you sponsor, and which tokens you accept.

## Webhook configuration

Register a webhook URL where the deposit service sends notifications about deposit events. You can optionally provide a secret for HMAC-SHA256 signature verification on incoming webhook payloads.

## Gas and fee sponsorship

Control which costs you cover for your users on a per-chain basis. You can sponsor gas fees, bridging fees, swap fees, or any combination. Unsponsored fees are deducted from the deposit amount.

## Deposit whitelist

Restrict which source chains and tokens the service accepts deposits from. You can also set minimum and maximum deposit amounts per token. Deposits outside the whitelist are ignored.
