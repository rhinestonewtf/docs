---
title: "Sponsorship"
description: "Sponsor deposit fees on behalf of your users."
---

The deposit service supports fee sponsorship, allowing you to cover some or all of the deposit fees on behalf of your users. This is useful for onboarding flows, promotional campaigns, or providing a seamless deposit experience.

## How it works

When sponsorship is enabled, the service deducts fees from your sponsorship balance instead of the deposit amount. Users receive the full deposit value on the target chain.

## Configuring sponsorship

Sponsorship is configured per project through the dashboard. You can set limits on total sponsored volume, per-deposit caps, and eligible chains or tokens.

## Monitoring usage

Track your sponsorship balance and usage history via the dashboard or the API. See the [API reference](/api-reference) for details.
